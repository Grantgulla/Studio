WORKWEAR
Abiti Da Lavoro presents
Feb 5th - April 18th
Opening reception Thursday, February 4, 6:00-8:00 p.m.
Workwear by Abiti da Lavoro is an exhibition of
experimental garments,
"work clothes" made for imaginary professions
and dedicated to revolutionary Italian fashion designer Elio Fiorucci (1935-2015).
Curated by Alessandro Guerriero.
Sheila C. Johnson Design Center
Designers
Nathalie Pasquier
Elio Fiorucci
Mella Jaarsma
Guda Koster
Toshiyuki Kita
Issey Miyake
Angela Missoni
Faye Toogood
Vivienne Westwood
Erwin Wurm
WORKWEAR was designed by Manuel Miranda Practice and created with the support of the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and The Italian Cultural Institute in New York. Curatorial support was provided by Associazione Tam Tam.
